Pancakes

Description

Follow this recipe to concoct the fluffiest, most delectable pancakes for the whole family to enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablesppon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1.

    In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well
    the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle,
    using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
