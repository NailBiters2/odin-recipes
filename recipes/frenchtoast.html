French Toast

Description

Follow this recipe to assemble the most scrumptous, mouth-watering French Toast that'll make your morning something special.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices of bread

Description

  1.

    Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt,
    eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.

  2. Heat a loghtly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.

  3.

    Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden
    brown. Serve hot.
