Waffles

Description

Follow this recipe and you'll end up with a batch of crispy and succulent waffles that make the start of your day something to look forward to and enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 table spoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cups butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

    2.

    In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.

    3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour rhe milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.

    4. Ladle the butter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.

    Serve immediately.
